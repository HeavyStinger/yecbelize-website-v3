Who we are

One of Belize's leading providers of high-quality civil and structural engineering — making a real difference in the lives of ordinary Belizeans.

Since our founding in 2000, Young's Engineering has delivered a wide range of projects spanning the length and breadth of Belize — public and private buildings, streets, drainage, water supply and distribution, and much more.

We pair internationally trained, registered engineers with deep local knowledge of Belize's ground, climate, and communities — so the work stands up, on budget and on time.

Leadership

Decades of engineering, registered on two coasts.

Carlton Young holds a bachelor's in civil engineering and a master's in structural engineering, both from the University of California, Berkeley, and is a registered professional engineer in both the United States and Belize.

He has worked on a wide variety of projects around the world and across Belize for more than twenty years, and is fluent in English and Spanish. Behind him stands a team of engineers, inspectors and technicians who carry that standard onto every site.
