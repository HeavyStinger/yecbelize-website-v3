City Streets & Park Upgrade

YEC provided civil and structural engineering for a major overhaul of the historic Fort Point area of Belize City — upgrades to streets and drainage, augmented parking, and new wide sidewalks. The centrepiece was a make-over of historic Memorial Park, including a modern stage with exposed large-span trusses and a curved retaining-wall seating structure incorporating an elegant memorial to Belizean war dead. Performed with our partner Sumathi Courtenay and Vividarch Company.
