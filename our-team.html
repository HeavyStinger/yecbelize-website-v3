The people behind the drawings.

Registered engineers, site inspectors, and technicians who carry one standard onto every site in Belize.

Principal

Carlton N. Young, P.E.

Principal · Project Manager · Engineer

Carlton is the Principal of the firm, responsible for all engineering work and holding ultimate responsibility for supervision and management. He holds a bachelor's in civil engineering and a master's in structural engineering, both from UC Berkeley, and is a registered professional engineer in the United States and Belize. He has worked on a wide variety of projects around the world and in Belize for more than twenty years, and is fluent in English and Spanish.

Emory K. Bennett

Site Inspector · Projects Coordinator

Emory coordinates projects, monitors construction works and progress, interfaces with contractor personnel, and runs testing and quality-assurance programs. He holds an associate's in physics and mathematics from Belize Technical College and a bachelor's in business administration from the University of Belize, with training in construction technologies.

Stephanie Coba

Civil Engineer

Stephanie performs civil and structural engineering calculations and provides site inspections and construction quality monitoring. She holds a bachelor's in civil engineering from Instituto Tecnológico de Chetumal and has practiced civil and structural engineering in Belize for several years.

Kenrick Munnings

Civil Engineering Technician

Kenrick coordinates the production of AutoCAD drawings, takes site measurements and levels, and prepares preliminary engineering layouts under the direction of the engineer. He holds an associate's degree in architecture from the University of Belize and has worked for 20 years with YEC.

Jahmaal Harris

AutoCAD Technician

Jahmaal produces AutoCAD drawings and assists with site measurements, taking levels, and performing quantity estimates.

Sylvia Garoy

Administrative Manager

Sylvia is responsible for all administrative management, including the preparation of reports and proposals, managing files and records, tax filing, and bookkeeping. She holds a bachelor's in business administration from the University of Belize and has worked with YEC for more than 10 years.

Inga Miguel

Front Desk Receptionist

Inga is our first line of communication — answering phones, transferring calls, and scheduling appointments. She also helps manage the flow of the office and looks after customer service.
